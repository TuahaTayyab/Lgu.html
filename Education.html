Education at LGU

Academic Programs

LGU offers a broad range of undergraduate and graduate programs across faculties including Computer Sciences, Basic Sciences, Social Sciences, Languages, and Management Sciences.

For example, in the Biology Department, they run PhD, MPhil, BS, and ADP (Associate Degree Programme) in specializations such as Microbiology, Zoology, Biochemistry, Biotechnology, Nutrition & Dietetics, and more.

Their Computer Science faculty includes BS, MS, and PhD level programs in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology, and related fields.
